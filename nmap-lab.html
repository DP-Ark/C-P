🛡️ Nmap Hands-on Lab

This page documents my hands-on practice with Nmap, a powerful network scanning tool used by cybersecurity professionals.

🎯 Lab Goals

  • Understand how to run basic port scans
  • Explore Nmap output and interpret results

🧑‍💻 Step-by-Step Commands

  1. Open Terminal
  2. Run: nmap scanme.nmap.org
  3. Wait for the scan to complete
  4. Review the open ports and service names

📝 Observations

I scanned scanme.nmap.org and observed open ports such as 22 (SSH), 80 (HTTP), and 443 (HTTPS).
